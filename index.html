Snake

  • Control the orange snake's movement with the arrow keys.
  • Collide with as many green food pellets as you can without colliding with a wall or with the snake's body.
  • Pellets make your snake grow. The game ends when you collide with a wall or the snake, and your score is based on the length of the snake at game's end.
  • The game is in solitaire mode by default, but can be switched to competitive mode via the toggle above.
  • A second player can control the blue snake with the WASD keys.
  • In competitive mode, the game ends when either snake experiences a collision with a wall, with the other snake, or with themselves.
